Aboutme

Boitumelo 'Shakex' Sempe is an International Relations graduate and freelance Front-End Web Developer specializing in responsive landing and portfolio webpages suited for small businesses and individuals alike.

I obtained my Bachelors degree (Bpolsci) from the University of Pretoria having gained my A-levels at the International School of South Africa

I worked as a Customer Care agent at the Compensation Fund (Department of Labour) and in 2019 enrolled in the online coding bootcamp freeCodeCamp where I completed a certificate in Responsive Web Design.

I code responsive web pages suitable for devices ranging from small mobile screens to large desktop screens. My preferred tool for building and maintaining stylesheets is Sass while I use HTML Semantic Markup and On-Page Search Engine Optimization techniques to grow website views, essential for online presence and web traffic to the site by potential customers and clients.

I am currently learning the MERN stack which I aim to build full stack web products.
